My story

Curiosity turned lifelong craft.

Photography has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. My dad always had a camera in hand and my sister studied the art in college—I caught the bug early and never put the lens down.

I picked up my first camera at 13 and dove into every genre: sports, portraits, landscapes, street, events, you name it. The challenge has always been the same—find the emotion and lock it in with clarity and style.

Today I’m the official photographer for Stalybridge Celtic Football Club. Capturing the energy of match days, the emotion of the players, and the spirit of the fans keeps me pushing creatively on every shoot.

Approach

Collaborative, prepared, and adaptable. I plan meticulously and leave room for the moments you can’t script.
