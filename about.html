My Story

Photography has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. My dad always had a camera in hand, capturing everyday moments, and I was fascinated by the process from a young age. I always wanted to join in, and that curiosity only grew when my sister studied photography at college, her passion and creativity sparked something in me that never faded.

I picked up my first camera at age 13, and what began as a hobby quickly became a lifelong obsession. Over the years, I've explored all forms of photography, from sports and portraits to landscapes, street scenes, and events.

I believe photography is a constantly evolving art, and I'm committed to pushing my creative boundaries and staying inspired.

Today, I am the official photographer for Stalybridge Celtic Football Club. Capturing the energy of match days, the emotion of the players, and the spirit of the fans. My goal is to tell compelling visual stories that resonate with people, whether on the pitch or beyond.

Photography, for me, is more than just taking pictures it's about preserving moments, sharing perspectives, and connecting with others through the lens.
