Frequently Asked Questions

What's your photography style?

I specialize in a blend of photojournalistic and artistic styles, capturing authentic moments while creating visually stunning compositions. My approach is natural and unobtrusive, allowing genuine emotions to shine through.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and major events, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For portrait sessions and smaller projects, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient, though I can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely! I love destination photography and am available for travel worldwide. Travel expenses are typically included in the project quote for locations outside the local area.

What's included in your packages?

Each package is customized to your specific needs, but typically includes pre-shoot consultation, the photography session, professional editing, and delivery of high-resolution images via online gallery.

How long until I receive my photos?

Turnaround time varies by project type. Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 1-2 weeks, while weddings and larger events may take 4-6 weeks for complete editing and delivery.

Do you offer prints?

Yes! I offer professional printing services through trusted labs, including canvas prints, albums, and fine art prints. These can be discussed during our consultation.
